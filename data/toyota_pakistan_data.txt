=== TOYOTA MODEL COMPARISONS ===

YARIS vs COROLLA ALTIS:
- Yaris: Smaller, cheaper (PKR 4.6M-5.9M), 1.3-1.5L engine, 155mm ground clearance, better fuel economy, ideal for city
- Corolla Altis: Larger, more premium (PKR 6.3M-8.0M), 1.6-1.8L engine, more power, better features, ideal for highway + families
- Key difference: Corolla is bigger (4630mm vs 4425mm), has more boot space, and feels more premium
- Recommendation: Yaris for tight budget/city use; Corolla for long-distance or family sedan

PETROL vs HYBRID (Corolla Cross):
- Petrol sedan (Altis): Lower purchase price, simpler maintenance, parts easily available
- Hybrid (Cross): Higher purchase price but saves 30-40% on fuel, better for high-mileage drivers
- Break-even: If you drive 2000+ km/month, hybrid pays back in 3-4 years
- Hybrid battery warranty: 8 years / 160,000 km

BEST FOR FAMILY:
- Budget family: Toyota Yaris ATIV X (PKR 5.7M) — 5 seats, good safety, CVT option
- Mid-range family: Toyota Corolla Altis Grande (PKR 8.0M) — premium, safe, comfortable
- Large family: Toyota Fortuner (PKR 13M+) — 7 seats, 3 rows, SUV safety
- Best overall value family car: Toyota Corolla Altis 1.6 CVT

BEST FUEL EFFICIENCY (ranked):
1. Toyota Corolla Cross Hybrid — 20-26 km/L
2. Toyota Yaris 1.3 MT — 16-21 km/L
3. Toyota Yaris 1.3 CVT — 15-20 km/L
4. Toyota Corolla Altis 1.6 MT — 13-18 km/L
5. Toyota Fortuner 2.8 Diesel — 10-15 km/L
6. Toyota Fortuner 2.7 Petrol — 8-13 km/L

BEST VALUE FOR MONEY:
- Under PKR 5M: Toyota Yaris GLI MT
- Under PKR 7M: Toyota Corolla Altis 1.6 MT
- Under PKR 11M: Toyota Corolla Cross Hybrid
- Family SUV: Toyota Fortuner 2.7 (better value than diesel)

BEST FOR LONG-DISTANCE TRAVEL:
1. Toyota Fortuner — High ground clearance, powerful engine, 7 seats
2. Toyota Corolla Altis Grande — Comfortable, fuel-efficient highway cruiser
3. Toyota Corolla Cross Hybrid — Best fuel economy in SUV body style

MODELS WITH TOYOTA SAFETY SENSE (TSS):
- Toyota Corolla Cross Hybrid (standard)
- Toyota Fortuner Legender (standard)
- Toyota Hilux GRS (standard)
- Note: TSS includes Pre-Collision System, Lane Departure Alert, Radar Cruise Control

AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION AVAILABILITY:
- Toyota Yaris: Available (CVT on ATIV and ATIV X variants)

=== BUYING GUIDE FOR PAKISTAN ===

QUESTIONS TO ASK BEFORE BUYING:
1. What is your monthly driving distance?
2. Is it primarily city or highway use?
3. How many passengers regularly?
4. What is your total budget (include registration, insurance)?
5. Do you prefer automatic or manual?

REGISTRATION COSTS (approximate):
- Yaris: PKR 200,000-300,000
- Corolla Altis: PKR 350,000-500,000
- Corolla Cross: PKR 600,000-800,000
- Fortuner: PKR 900,000-1,500,000

INSURANCE (annual estimate):
- Comprehensive: 2-3% of vehicle value
- Tracker installation recommended for all models

TIPS FOR TOYOTA BUYERS IN PAKISTAN:
- Always buy from authorized IMC dealers
- Check delivery time (Fortuner can have 6-12 month wait)
- Verify Corolla Cross hybrid certification for tax benefits
- Request PDI (Pre-Delivery Inspection) report
- Check for any active recalls on IMC website

CNG COMPATIBILITY:
- Yaris: Supports CNG (aftermarket kit)
- Corolla Altis: Supports CNG (1.6L, not recommended on 1.8L)
- Fortuner: NOT recommended for CNG
- Corolla Cross Hybrid: NOT compatible with CNG
